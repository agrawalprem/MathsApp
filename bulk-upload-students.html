Bulk Upload Students

Upload multiple students at once. Students will be created with auto-generated accounts.

Instructions:
  1. Prepare your student data in CSV format (see template below)
  2. Choose password option (auto-generate or default password)
  3. Paste CSV data or upload file
  4. Click "Upload Students"
  5. Download the password file to share with students
CSV Format: student_id,first_name,last_name,gender,school_id,class,section,roll_number
